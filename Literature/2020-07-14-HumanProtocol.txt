Sahar + Brandon "IIIC treatment protocol" 

Two cases: 
1. NCSE - nonconvulsive status epilepticus [ > 50% of a 30min epoch = sz/IIIC]
2. NCS - nonconvulsive seizures [<50% of a 30 min epoch = sz/IIIC ]

--------------------------------------------
Suggested protocol for NCSE

1. Minutes 5-10: 
-Lorazepam (or diazepam or midazolam) bolus

2. Minutes 10-30: if NCSE continues...
-Bolus NSAED ("loading dose"), and prescribe NSAED to be given q8-12h
-wait 60 min, if NCSE persists: 
--increase dose of NSAED
--Bolus NSAED #2 ("loading dose") and prescribe NSAED to be given q8-12h

3. Minutes 60-90min: if NCSE continues: 
-bolus SAED (anesthetic drug), titrate to achieve sz suppression or burst suppression (but see below)
-increase NSAEDs to maximal doses
(--Bolus NSAED #3 ("loading dose") and prescribe NSAED to be given q8-12h)

--------------------------------------------
Suggested protocol for NCS

1. Minutes 5-10: 
-Lorazepam (or diazepam or midazolam) bolus

2. Minutes 10-30: if NCSE continues...
-Bolus NSAED ("loading dose"), and prescribe NSAED to be given q8-12h
-wait 60 min, if NCSE persists: 
--increase dose of NSAED
--Bolus NSAED #2 ("loading dose") and prescribe NSAED to be given q8-12h

3. After 8h: if NCS continue
--Bolus NSAED #3 ("loading dose") and prescribe NSAED to be given q8-12h

--------------------------------------------
NOTES/CONSTRAINTS: 

1. Titration of SAEDs:
--once started, continue x 24h (targeting sz suppression or burst suppression)
--adjust infusion rate hourly as needed
--after 24h taper over 12h (decreasing rate hourly until off)
--if NCSE recurs, repeat 24h of anesthetics
--repeat up to 3x (takes ~4.5 days)
--do no exceed max dose [propofol 10mg/kg/h; mdz: 2mg/kg/h]

2. Titration of NSAEDs:
--can increase dose every 8h; increase if sz >2h after last dose
--stop increasing when at max dose (up to 1 increase); we can assume there are 2 dose options for each drug
